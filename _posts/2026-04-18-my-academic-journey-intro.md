## 🌟 The Beginning

Every journey has a starting point. Mine began in a small nursery classroom, not knowing that years later I'd be writing code and analyzing data. This is the first post in my academic journey series documenting everything from my earliest school days to becoming a Software Engineering student.

## 🏫 Why I'm Writing This

I believe every student's journey is unique. By sharing mine, I hope to:

- **Inspire** other students facing similar challenges in their education
- **Document** the reality of what a Software Engineering education actually looks like
- **Share** resources, books, and websites that genuinely helped me learn
- **Connect** with fellow learners who are on similar paths

## 📚 What to Expect in This Series

### Part 1: Early Years (Nursery to Primary)
My very first classroom experiences, learning to read and write, and how my curiosity for understanding how things work first emerged.

### Part 2: Secondary School Years

### Part 3: University Life - Software Engineering
The real journey begins here. I'll break down:
- My first year experiences and adjustments
- **Every course unit** I'm taking semester by semester
- Projects I'm building (including my data science work)

### Part 4: Lessons Learned Along The Way
What study strategies actually worked for me, what I would do differently if I started over, and honest advice for future engineering students.

## 🎯 What I'm Currently Working On

Alongside my coursework, I'm actively building:

- **Life Expectancy Analysis** - Using Python and data science to understand what factors affect how long people live in different countries
- **Internet Usage Prediction Model** - A machine learning project to predict internet adoption rates
- **React Dashboard Systems** - Building interactive web applications for visualizing data
- **IoT & Embedded Systems** - Working with hardware, sensors, and microcontrollers
